About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning attractions.

Attractions

Hyderabad is home to iconic landmarks such as the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Hussain Sagar Lake.

History

Hyderabad has a fascinating history, from its founding by the Qutb Shahi dynasty to its later rule by the Nizams.

Culture

The culture of Hyderabad is a blend of traditional South Indian, Islamic, and modern influences, evident in its cuisine, festivals, and architecture.
